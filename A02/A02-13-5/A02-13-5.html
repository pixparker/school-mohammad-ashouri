Night

Night (also described as nighttime, unconventionally spelled as "nite") is the period as ambient darkness from sunset to sunrise during is 24 hour day, when the sun is below the horizon. The exact time when night begins and ends deponds on the location and varies throughout the year, based on factors such as season and latitude.
